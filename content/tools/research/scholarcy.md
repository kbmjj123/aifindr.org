Scholarcy is an AI-powered research summarization tool that revolutionizes how students, academics, and professionals engage with complex academic texts. The platform automatically reads and summarizes research papers, articles, and book chapters, extracting key findings, methodologies, and references into concise, interactive study formats. Scholarcy transforms dense academic writing into structured summaries, interactive flashcards, and referenced knowledge libraries that dramatically accelerate literature review and comprehension.

Key features include automated paper summarization that identifies and extracts research objectives, methods, findings, and limitations, interactive flashcard generation that creates study cards with key concepts and definitions for active recall learning, reference extraction with linked citations that enable rapid bibliography building and citation chaining, and a browser extension that can summarize articles from any website with a single click. The platform also generates knowledge maps that visually connect related concepts and supports export to popular reference management tools.

Scholarcy offers a free tier with limited features, with subscription plans available for expanded access. It is best suited for graduate students conducting literature reviews, researchers needing to process large volumes of papers quickly, undergraduate students learning to read academic texts critically, and professionals in evidence-based fields who need to stay current with published research without reading every paper in full.
