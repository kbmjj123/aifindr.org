Coris is an AI-powered risk intelligence platform that automates merchant screening, fraud detection, and business verification for financial institutions, payment processors, and marketplace platforms. The platform uses machine learning to analyze merchant profiles, transaction patterns, and business documentation, identifying potential fraud, compliance violations, and reputational risks before they impact the business. Coris replaces manual merchant review processes with intelligent automation that is faster, more consistent, and more thorough.

Key features include automated merchant onboarding that verifies business identity, beneficial ownership, and regulatory compliance in real time, continuous monitoring that flags changes in merchant behavior, risk profiles, or regulatory status, AI-driven fraud pattern detection that identifies suspicious activity across merchant portfolios, and customizable risk scoring calibrated to each organization's risk tolerance and compliance requirements. The platform also generates audit-ready documentation trails and integrates with existing underwriting and compliance workflows.

Coris offers customized enterprise pricing based on business needs and review volume, with a demo required to determine specific requirements and pricing. It is best suited for payment processors and acquirers managing merchant portfolios, fintech platforms onboarding business customers, online marketplaces screening sellers, and financial institutions needing to automate their Know Your Business (KYB) and anti-fraud processes while maintaining regulatory compliance.
