Patlytics is an AI-driven intellectual property platform that optimizes the entire patent lifecycle, from initial creation and filing through protection, portfolio management, and litigation support. The platform uses advanced natural language processing and machine learning to analyze patent documents, prior art, and competitive landscapes, helping IP professionals make smarter, faster decisions about patent strategy. Patlytics transforms the traditionally labor-intensive patent workflow into an efficient, data-driven process.

Key features include AI-powered prior art search that rapidly identifies relevant existing patents and publications, automated patent drafting assistance that generates claims and specification sections based on invention disclosures, competitive landscape analysis that maps patent portfolios across companies and technology areas, and litigation intelligence that predicts outcomes and identifies relevant case law. The platform also provides portfolio valuation tools, infringement detection, and monitoring for new filings in specified technology domains.

Patlytics offers customized enterprise pricing based on organizational needs, with subscription plans tailored to patent volume and user count. It is best suited for patent attorneys and IP law firms managing multiple client portfolios, corporate IP departments optimizing their patent strategy, technology companies building defensive and offensive patent positions, and investors conducting IP due diligence for mergers and acquisitions.
