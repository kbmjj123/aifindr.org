Perplexity is an AI-powered search and answer engine that transforms how people find information online. Unlike traditional search engines that return links, Perplexity generates direct answers with citations, synthesized from multiple sources in real-time.

The platform combines web search with large language models to understand complex queries and provide comprehensive, well-sourced answers. Users can ask follow-up questions, explore related topics, and organize their research into collections. The Pro version offers access to multiple AI models and advanced analysis capabilities.

Perplexity has gained significant traction as a research tool for professionals, students, and anyone who wants accurate, cited information quickly.
