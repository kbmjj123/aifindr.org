In the ever-evolving landscape of digital tools aimed at enhancing our ability to gather, process, and understand vast amounts of information, Consensus emerges as a beacon for researchers, students, professionals, and anyone in pursuit of knowledge. At its core, Consensus is an AI-powered search engine designed specifically for research purposes. Unlike traditional search engines that often return a barrage of loosely related information, Consensus zeroes in on delivering insights from over 200 million scientific papers. Its mission is straightforward yet profound: to democratize access to expert knowledge by enabling users to conduct efficient, reliable research without the clutter of ads or the necessity of keyword matching.
