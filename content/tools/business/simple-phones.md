Simple Phones is an AI-powered business phone solution that ensures no customer call goes unanswered. The platform uses natural language voice AI to create intelligent phone agents that can answer inbound calls, qualify leads, schedule appointments, answer frequently asked questions, and route callers to the appropriate team member. Businesses can set up a custom AI phone agent in minutes, defining the agent's voice, personality, knowledge base, and call-handling logic through an intuitive configuration interface.

Key features include instant AI agent creation with customizable voice and behavior, intelligent call routing that understands caller intent and directs accordingly, automated appointment scheduling that integrates with popular calendar systems, call transcription and summarization for follow-up and record-keeping, and multi-language support for serving diverse customer bases. The platform also provides detailed call analytics, missed call recovery that follows up with text messages, and the ability to handle multiple simultaneous calls with no wait times.

Simple Phones starts with a 14-day free trial, with the Standard plan at $49 per month for up to 100 calls. It is best suited for small businesses, service providers, real estate agents, medical practices, and any organization that receives customer calls but lacks the staff or budget for a full-time receptionist or call center.
