Gong is a revenue intelligence platform that uses AI to analyze customer interactions across calls, emails, and meetings. It automatically captures, transcribes, and analyzes sales conversations to provide actionable insights for revenue teams.

The AI identifies winning sales behaviors, risk signals, competitive mentions, and objection handling patterns across thousands of conversations. Gong provides deal forecasting, coaching recommendations, and competitive intelligence based on actual customer conversations rather than manual data entry.

Gong is widely adopted by enterprise sales teams who want to base their sales strategy on data-driven insights derived from real customer interactions.
