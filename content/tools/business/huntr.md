Huntr is an AI-powered job search management platform that helps job seekers streamline every aspect of their application process. The tool acts as a comprehensive career command center where users can track job applications, optimize resumes and cover letters for specific roles, manage contacts and networking activities, and monitor their overall search progress. Huntr's browser extension captures job listings from any website with a single click, automatically populating key details into the user's tracking board.

Key features include a visual Kanban-style application tracker with customizable stages from "Saved" to "Offer Received," AI-powered resume analysis that provides keyword optimization suggestions tailored to specific job descriptions, cover letter templates and generation tools, a contacts and networking manager for tracking recruiter and referral conversations, and analytics dashboards showing application funnel metrics like response rates and interview conversion. The platform also includes reminders for follow-ups, interview preparation tools, and a Chrome extension for one-click job saving.

Huntr offers a free tier to get started, with premium features available through paid subscriptions. It is best suited for active job seekers across all career stages, career coaches helping multiple clients, recent graduates entering the workforce, and professionals making career transitions who need an organized, data-driven approach to managing their job search.
