LivePerson is a leading enterprise conversational AI platform that revolutionizes how businesses interact with customers across messaging, voice, and digital channels. The platform enables organizations to deploy AI-powered chatbots and virtual agents that handle customer inquiries, automate routine service tasks, and seamlessly escalate complex issues to human agents when needed. LivePerson's conversational intelligence engine analyzes customer intent in real time, routing conversations intelligently and providing agents with contextual recommendations.

Key features include Intent Manager for real-time conversation analysis and routing, Conversational AI builder tools for creating sophisticated chatbots without deep coding expertise, Agent Workspace for unified omnichannel customer engagement across messaging apps, SMS, web chat, and voice, and comprehensive analytics dashboards that measure conversation outcomes and customer satisfaction. The platform also supports integrations with major CRM, e-commerce, and contact center systems for end-to-end workflow automation.

LivePerson offers customized enterprise pricing based on business needs and conversation volume, with no publicly listed flat-rate plans. It is best suited for large enterprises with significant customer service operations, retail and e-commerce brands seeking conversational commerce capabilities, financial services firms requiring secure messaging, and telecommunications companies managing high-volume customer interactions across multiple channels.
