Navina is an AI-powered clinical intelligence platform that transforms chaotic, fragmented healthcare data into structured, actionable insights for clinicians. Designed for physicians, nurse practitioners, and care teams, Navina ingests data from electronic health records, lab results, imaging reports, specialist notes, and payer information, then uses machine learning to synthesize a comprehensive, organized patient portrait. This enables clinicians to make faster, more informed decisions at the point of care without spending hours manually reviewing scattered records.

Key features include AI-driven patient summarization that surfaces the most relevant clinical information for each encounter, risk stratification tools that identify patients needing preventive care or chronic disease management, quality gap analysis that highlights missed care opportunities against evidence-based guidelines, and seamless EHR integration that fits into existing clinical workflows. The platform also supports value-based care initiatives by helping practices close care gaps and improve quality measure performance.

Navina offers custom pricing based on the specific needs and scale of healthcare organizations, with a free tier available for initial evaluation. It is best suited for primary care practices, multi-specialty medical groups, accountable care organizations, and health systems seeking to reduce clinician burnout while improving patient outcomes through better data accessibility and clinical decision support.
