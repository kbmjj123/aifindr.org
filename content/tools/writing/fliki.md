Fliki is an AI-powered text-to-video platform that converts written content into engaging videos with lifelike voiceovers. Used by content creators, marketers, and educators, it combines text-to-speech synthesis with a rich media library to produce professional videos in minutes — no video editing experience needed.

## Key Features
- **Text-to-Video**: Paste a blog post, script, or social media caption and Fliki automatically generates a video with matching visuals, captions, and transitions
- **AI Voiceovers**: Choose from 2,000+ realistic AI voices across 75+ languages with adjustable speed, pitch, and emotion
- **Media Library**: Access millions of stock images, video clips, and background music tracks integrated directly into the editor
- **Brand Customization**: Add brand colors, logos, fonts, and intros/outros for consistent branded video content

## Who Is It For
Content creators producing YouTube shorts and social media videos, marketers creating product demos and ads, and educators turning lesson materials into video tutorials.
