Regie is an AI-powered sales engagement platform that automates prospecting and content creation for outbound sales teams. It combines generative AI with sales workflows to help reps write personalized emails, sequences, and call scripts at scale.

## Key Features
- **AI Email Writer**: Generate personalized cold outreach emails using prospect data, company info, and industry context for higher reply rates
- **Sequence Builder**: Create multi-touch sales sequences with AI-generated messaging tailored to each persona and funnel stage
- **Content Library**: Access and customize proven sales templates, scripts, and playbooks
- **CRM Integration**: Native integrations with Salesforce, HubSpot, and Outreach to sync contacts and track performance

## Who Is It For
SDRs and BDRs who send high volumes of outreach, sales managers looking to standardize messaging across teams, and revenue operations teams wanting to leverage AI for pipeline generation.
