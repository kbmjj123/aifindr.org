JustCopy AI is a content creation platform that helps founders, marketers, and developers ship production-ready landing pages and marketing copy using AI-powered templates. Instead of staring at a blank page, users pick from proven copy templates and let the AI generate tailored content in seconds.

## Key Features
- **Template Library**: Pre-built templates for landing pages, email campaigns, ad copy, and product descriptions — based on high-converting copy patterns
- **AI Content Generation**: Describe your product and target audience; the AI generates complete copy optimized for your use case
- **Customizable Outputs**: Edit, tweak, and iterate on AI-generated copy with inline editing and version history
- **Multi-Format Export**: Export copy as HTML, Markdown, or plain text ready for deployment

## Who Is It For
Indie hackers launching products, marketing teams needing high-volume copy, and developers who want to ship landing pages without writing content from scratch.
