BeeBee is an AI earnings call and financial-report analysis tool aimed at investors who care about fundamentals. It digests conference call transcripts and formal filings, then surfaces risks, opportunities, key numbers, sentiment, and management strategy in structured form. On top of that, it ranks companies and CEOs, tracks top stock movers, and curates Q&A so users can understand why a stock moved without wading through every page of raw documents.
