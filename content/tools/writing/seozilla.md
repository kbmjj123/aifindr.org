SEOZilla is an AI-powered SEO content platform that auto-generates long-form blog posts optimized for search engines. It handles everything from keyword research to drafting and publishing, making it a complete content automation solution for SEO-driven marketing teams.

## Key Features
- **AI Blog Generation**: Input a target keyword and SEOZilla generates a complete, well-structured blog post with headings, meta descriptions, and internal links
- **Keyword Research**: Built-in keyword analysis to identify ranking opportunities and content gaps
- **Auto-Publishing**: Schedule and automatically publish content to WordPress, Webflow, and other CMS platforms
- **Content Optimization**: Real-time SEO scoring and suggestions to improve on-page factors like readability, keyword density, and structure

## Who Is It For
SEO agencies managing multiple client blogs, content marketers who need consistent publishing cadence, and SaaS companies building organic traffic through content marketing.
