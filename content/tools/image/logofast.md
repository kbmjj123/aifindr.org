LogoFast is a free AI logo maker that lets anyone create professional-quality logos in seconds — no design skills required. Built on NextJS, it generates SVG and PNG logos using AI-driven layout and style suggestions.

## Key Features
- **AI Logo Generation**: Describe your brand and the AI generates multiple logo variations with customizable colors, fonts, and layouts
- **Free Tier**: Create and download low-resolution logos for free, perfect for prototyping and early-stage startups
- **Pro Tier**: High-resolution logo downloads with full commercial rights starting at $39 per logo
- **Instant Export**: Download logos in SVG and PNG formats, ready for websites, social media, and print

## Why LogoFast?
Unlike complex design tools like Canva or Illustrator, LogoFast strips away the learning curve. You type a brand name, pick a style, and get usable logos immediately — making it ideal for indie hackers, startup founders, and small business owners who need a logo right now.
