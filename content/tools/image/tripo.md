Tripo is an AI-powered 3D model generator that transforms text descriptions or 2D images into detailed, production-ready 3D assets in seconds. Built for game developers, 3D artists, and designers, Tripo eliminates the hours of manual modeling typically required for 3D content creation.

## Key Features
- **Text-to-3D**: Describe an object and Tripo generates a textured 3D mesh — from characters and props to architectural elements
- **Image-to-3D**: Upload a reference photo and the AI reconstructs it as a 3D model with accurate proportions and textures
- **Multiple Export Formats**: Download models in GLB, FBX, OBJ, and USDZ formats compatible with Blender, Unity, Unreal Engine, and other 3D tools
- **Fast Generation**: Most models generate in under 30 seconds, making rapid iteration possible

## Pricing
Paid plans with API access for developers and enterprise users needing high-volume 3D generation.

## Who Is It For
Game developers, VR/AR creators, product designers, and 3D artists who want to accelerate their asset pipeline with AI-generated models.
