Spiritt is an AI-powered app builder that empowers startups to plan, build, and launch applications without writing code. Combining AI-assisted planning with visual prototyping, Spiritt helps founders move from idea to functional product faster than traditional development.

## Key Features
- **AI Project Planning**: Describe your app idea and Spiritt generates a structured project plan with feature breakdowns, user flows, and technical recommendations
- **Visual App Builder**: Drag-and-drop interface with pre-built components for common app patterns — authentication, profiles, feeds, and dashboards
- **Market Analysis Tools**: Built-in tools to analyze competitors and validate market fit before committing development resources
- **Networking & Collaboration**: Connect with other founders, share projects, and collaborate in real-time

## Pricing
Free tier gives access to basic planning and building features. Premium tier from $29.99/month unlocks advanced analytics, team collaboration, and priority support.

## Who Is It For
Early-stage startup founders, indie hackers, and product managers who want to prototype and validate ideas without hiring a development team.
