Promptomania is an innovative online platform designed to enhance the experience of creating AI-generated art. It serves as a prompt generator tailored specifically for AI art communities, supporting a wide range of diffusion models, including CF Spark, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, DALL-E 2, Disco Diffusion, and WOMBO Dream. Its user-friendly interface caters to both novices and seasoned artists aiming to master the art of AI-driven image creation. The tool offers unique features like a grid splitter, which aids in managing and refining output images, making it a versatile choice for digital creators.
