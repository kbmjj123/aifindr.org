GeoEvident is an AI-powered image forensics tool that analyzes photos to pinpoint their geographic location. Designed for insurance adjusters, law enforcement, legal professionals, and field investigators, it combines computer vision with geospatial intelligence to provide verifiable location data from any image.

## Key Features
- **Location Detection**: AI analyzes visual cues, landmarks, and metadata to determine where a photo was taken
- **Audit-Ready Reports**: Generate professional reports with confidence scores, evidence markers, and chain-of-custody documentation
- **Batch Processing**: Upload and analyze multiple photos simultaneously for large-scale investigations
- **Metadata Extraction**: Reads EXIF, GPS, and timestamps from images to corroborate location findings

## Who Is It For
Insurance fraud investigators, law enforcement agencies, legal professionals handling evidence verification, and field service teams who need to verify photo authenticity and location claims.
