Dora is an AI-powered website builder that generates complete, visually stunning websites from simple text prompts. Built on a no-code canvas with 3D and animation capabilities, Dora lets designers and non-designers alike create production-ready sites without writing a single line of code.

## Key Features
- **Prompt-to-Site**: Describe what you want in natural language and Dora generates a complete responsive website with layout, typography, and imagery
- **3D & Animation**: Built-in support for 3D transforms, scroll-triggered animations, and interactive elements that rival hand-coded sites
- **No-Code Editor**: Drag-and-drop canvas with pixel-perfect controls for those who want to fine-tune AI-generated layouts
- **Free Tier**: Start with 120 free credits to generate and publish sites, no credit card required
- **Hosting Included**: One-click publish with Dora's built-in hosting, or export code for self-hosting

## Who Is It For
Designers who want to move fast, startup founders who need a landing page without hiring a developer, and creatives exploring AI-driven web design.
