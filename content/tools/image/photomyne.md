Photomyne is an AI-powered app suite that brings old physical photographs back to life. Using advanced computer vision and machine learning, it scans, digitizes, enhances, and restores printed photos, slides, and film negatives directly from your smartphone camera.

## Key Features

- **Photo Scanning**: Scan multiple photos at once — the AI automatically crops, rotates, and saves each one individually with perspective correction and glare removal
- **Photo Enhancement**: One-tap color restoration enhances faded colors, sharpens details, and removes dust, scratches, and other imperfections
- **Photo Animation**: Add motion to still photos, creating short animated clips with subtle facial movements and depth effects
- **Colorization**: Convert black and white photos to color using deep learning models that produce natural, lifelike results
- **Film & Slide Scanning**: Scan old film negatives and 35mm slides using the phone camera and convert them to digital positives

## Pricing

Photomyne offers a 3-day free trial. The yearly subscription is $59.99/year, granting access to the full suite of scanning and enhancement apps. Free tier users have limited access to basic scanning features.

## Platforms

Available on iOS and Android. Requires a smartphone with a camera.

## Who Is It For

Photomyne is ideal for anyone with boxes of old family photos, slides, or negatives who wants to preserve them digitally. Genealogists, family historians, and anyone looking to create a searchable digital archive of their family memories will find it especially useful.
