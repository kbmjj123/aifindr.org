Adobe Firefly is a family of generative AI models integrated directly into Adobe's creative applications. Unlike standalone AI image generators, Firefly is designed to work seamlessly within workflows that creative professionals already use.

Firefly offers text-to-image generation, generative fill for adding or removing objects in photos, text effects for stylized typography, generative recolor for vector artwork, and 3D-to-image capabilities. The models are trained on licensed content and public domain works, making them safe for commercial use.

Firefly is integrated across Photoshop, Illustrator, Adobe Express, and other Creative Cloud applications, bringing AI capabilities directly into professional creative workflows.
