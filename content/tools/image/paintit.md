Paintit is an AI interior design tool that helps users restyle rooms, test layouts, and preview furniture in realistic 3D visuals. By analyzing photos, sketches, or room dimensions, it proposes complete room concepts, suggests furniture that fits the space, and supports both quick experiments and more serious renovation planning. It targets homeowners, interior designers, real estate professionals, and businesses that want to refresh interiors or add virtual staging without hiring a full design team every time.
