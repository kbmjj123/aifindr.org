Motion is an AI-powered productivity platform that automatically plans your day. Unlike traditional to-do lists and calendar apps, Motion uses AI to schedule tasks based on their priority, estimated duration, deadlines, and your available time.

The platform combines project management, calendar scheduling, and task automation into a single system. AI intelligently adjusts your schedule when priorities change or new tasks are added, ensuring you always work on the most important things. It also provides analytics on how you spend your time.

Motion is designed for professionals who manage multiple projects and want to optimize their daily productivity without spending time planning their schedule manually.
