Notion AI is an integrated AI assistant that works across your entire Notion workspace. Unlike standalone AI writing tools, Notion AI operates directly within your documents, databases, and knowledge base.

The AI can help draft documents, summarize meeting notes, brainstorm ideas, fix spelling and grammar, translate content, and generate action items from notes. It has context about your workspace, making its suggestions more relevant to your specific projects and team.

Notion AI is particularly powerful for project documentation, meeting notes, product requirements, and knowledge base creation where the AI can pull context from related pages and databases.
