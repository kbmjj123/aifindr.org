Cheat Layer is a no-code AI automation platform that lets businesses build and deploy custom AI agents to handle repetitive tasks using natural language descriptions.

## Key Features
- **Natural Language Automation**: Describe what you want to automate in plain English, and Cheat Layer generates a working automation script
- **Pre-Built Agents**: Turnkey AI agents for lead qualification, invoice processing, and customer support triage
- **App Integrations**: Connect to 100+ tools including Gmail, Slack, Salesforce, Stripe, and Google Sheets
- **Enterprise Security**: SOC 2 compliant with on-premise deployment option for regulated industries

## Who Is It For
Operations teams tired of manual data entry, small businesses automating back-office work, and agencies building AI-powered workflows for clients.
