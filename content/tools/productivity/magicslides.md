MagicSlides is an AI presentation generator that turns ideas, documents, web links, or YouTube videos into fully designed, editable slide decks in seconds.

## Key Features
- **Multi-Source Input**: Generate presentations from a topic idea, blog post URL, PDF, or YouTube video
- **Auto-Design**: Professionally designed templates with automatic color schemes, font pairing, and layout
- **Editable Output**: Export to Google Slides or PowerPoint with full editing capability
- **Multi-Language**: Generate presentations in 100+ languages

## Who Is It For
Consultants preparing client decks, educators creating lecture materials, sales teams building pitch decks, and anyone who regularly makes presentations.
