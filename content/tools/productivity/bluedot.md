Bluedot is an innovative AI-powered notetaking tool specifically designed to enhance productivity during meetings on platforms like Google Meet and Microsoft Teams. Whether you're an entrepreneur, manager, or recruiter, Bluedot simplifies the process of capturing and sharing meeting insights, allowing users to focus more on meaningful interactions.
