DealSize.ai focuses on giving traders AI-driven market insight and trade analysis. The browser-based platform combines a chat-style assistant for technical analysis, a trade analytics module (ChartDS), and market utilities such as news and calendar. Traders can ask natural-language questions about forex pairs, commodities, crypto, and stocks, or review their own trading performance through broker connections. With a small specialist team and a clear “Made for Traders” identity, DealSize.ai aims to bring machine learning and real-time data closer to everyday trading workflows without requiring code or quant skills.
