Lido is an innovative AI-powered Optical Character Recognition (OCR) tool designed to streamline the conversion of PDFs and financial documents into Excel spreadsheets. Specifically targeted at finance and operations teams, Lido simplifies the traditionally cumbersome process of manual data entry by automating the extraction of data from PDFs, thereby saving time and reducing errors. This tool is particularly beneficial for professionals seeking efficiency in handling large volumes of financial documents.
