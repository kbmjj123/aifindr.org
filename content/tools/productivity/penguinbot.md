PenguinBot is an AI executive assistant that automates daily productivity — email triage, calendar scheduling, document drafting, and task management — through a conversational interface.

## Key Features
- **Email Management**: AI reads, categorizes, and drafts replies to your emails, reducing inbox time by 70%
- **Smart Scheduling**: Automatically finds meeting times across time zones, sends invites, and reschedules conflicts
- **Document Creation**: Generate reports, memos, and presentations from brief voice or text instructions
- **Task Execution**: Delegate multi-step workflows and PenguinBot handles the execution

## Who Is It For
Executives, founders, and busy professionals who spend too much time on administrative tasks and need a reliable AI assistant.
