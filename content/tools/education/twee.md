Twee is an innovative AI-powered tool designed specifically for English teachers. It revolutionizes the way educational content is created and personalized, offering a suite of features that streamline lesson planning and material preparation. With Twee, educators can generate questions for YouTube videos, create dialogues, stories, letters, or articles on any topic, and develop various exercises like multiple-choice questions, open questions, and true/false statements. Aimed at enhancing the learning and teaching experience, Twee simplifies the process of creating engaging and customized educational materials, saving educators a significant amount of time and effort.
