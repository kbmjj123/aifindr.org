Leya is an AI-powered English language learning platform that delivers personalized instruction focused on achieving genuine spoken fluency. Unlike generic language apps, Leya uses artificial intelligence to assess each learner's current proficiency level, identify specific pronunciation and grammar challenges, and create tailored lesson plans that target individual improvement areas. The platform emphasizes conversational practice and real-world communication skills over rote vocabulary memorization.

Key features include AI-driven speech assessment that provides detailed feedback on pronunciation, intonation, and fluency, personalized lesson pathways that adapt in real time based on learner performance and progress, interactive conversation simulations with AI tutors that mimic real-world dialogue scenarios, and comprehensive progress tracking covering vocabulary acquisition, grammatical accuracy, and speaking confidence. The platform also includes cultural context lessons and situational English practice for professional, academic, and social settings.

Leya offers a one-week free trial, with full access available at $19.99 per month. It is best suited for non-native English speakers preparing for professional environments, students aiming to improve academic English, immigrants seeking to build conversational confidence, and anyone who has studied English through traditional methods but struggles with spontaneous speaking fluency in real-world situations.
