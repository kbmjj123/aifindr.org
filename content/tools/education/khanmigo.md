Khanmigo is an AI-powered tutoring assistant developed by Khan Academy in partnership with OpenAI. Unlike traditional AI chatbots that simply provide answers, Khanmigo is designed to act as a Socratic tutor that guides students through problem-solving with thoughtful questions and hints.

The AI covers a wide range of subjects including mathematics, science, humanities, and computing. It adapts to each student's level and learning pace, providing personalized instruction. Khanmigo also includes features for teachers, such as lesson planning assistance, student progress tracking, and differentiation support.

Khanmigo represents one of the most thoughtful applications of AI in education, prioritizing pedagogical effectiveness over simply providing answers.
