Meta AI is the artificial intelligence research and development division of Meta (formerly Facebook), responsible for advancing the state of the art in machine learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and generative AI. Meta AI produces open-source models, tools, and research that power innovations across Meta's family of apps and are freely available to the global research community. The organization's flagship contributions include the LLaMA family of large language models, the Segment Anything Model for computer vision, and numerous research papers that advance foundational AI capabilities.

Key offerings include LLaMA open-source large language models available in multiple sizes for research and commercial use, the Segment Anything Model (SAM) for general-purpose image segmentation, Audiocraft for AI music and audio generation, and a suite of embodied AI and robotics research projects. Meta AI also develops and maintains PyTorch, one of the most widely used deep learning frameworks, and contributes significantly to open-source AI infrastructure. The organization provides extensive documentation, research papers, and code repositories to support the global AI community.

Meta AI tools and models are largely free and open source, supporting collaboration and innovation across academia and industry. The platform is best suited for AI researchers pushing the boundaries of machine learning, developers building applications powered by state-of-the-art models, data scientists exploring cutting-edge techniques, and organizations seeking to leverage or fine-tune foundation models for specialized use cases.
