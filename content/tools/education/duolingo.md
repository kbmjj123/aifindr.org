Duolingo is the world's most popular AI-powered language learning platform, used by hundreds of millions of learners globally. The platform uses artificial intelligence and machine learning to deliver gamified, personalized language lessons that adapt to each learner's proficiency level, learning pace, and knowledge gaps. Duolingo's AI engine, Birdbrain, models individual learner behavior to optimize lesson difficulty, predict when a user is likely to forget a concept, and schedule review sessions for maximum retention.

Key features include bite-sized interactive lessons covering reading, writing, listening, and speaking skills, an adaptive learning algorithm that personalizes exercise selection based on individual performance patterns, gamification elements including streaks, leaderboards, achievements, and a reward system that maintains learner motivation, and AI-powered speech recognition for pronunciation practice and feedback. The platform offers courses in over 40 languages, from widely spoken languages like Spanish and French to endangered and constructed languages. Duolingo also provides Duolingo English Test, an AI-proctored, affordable alternative to traditional English proficiency exams.

Duolingo offers a comprehensive free tier with full access to all language courses. Duolingo Super (formerly Plus) starts at $6.99 per month for an ad-free experience, offline lessons, unlimited hearts, and progress tracking. It is best suited for casual language learners, students supplementing classroom study, travelers preparing for trips, and anyone seeking an engaging, scientifically-validated approach to building language proficiency through consistent daily practice.
