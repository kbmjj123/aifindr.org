Duolingo Max is the next-generation language learning experience powered by OpenAI's GPT-4. It brings two major AI features to the already popular language learning platform: Explain My Answer and Roleplay.

Explain My Answer provides personalized feedback after each lesson, explaining why an answer was correct or incorrect in natural language. Roleplay allows learners to practice real-world conversations with AI characters in a safe, judgment-free environment. These AI features make language learning more interactive and personalized than traditional methods.

Duolingo remains the world's most popular language learning platform with over 500 million users, offering courses in 40+ languages with a gamified approach to education.
