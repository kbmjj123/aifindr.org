Veesual is an AI-powered virtual try-on platform that revolutionizes the online shopping experience for fashion and apparel retailers. The platform uses advanced computer vision and generative AI to create realistic visualizations of how clothing items will look on different body types, sizes, and poses. By enabling shoppers to see garments on models that reflect their own body characteristics, Veesual reduces return rates, increases purchase confidence, and bridges the gap between the convenience of online shopping and the certainty of in-store fitting rooms.

Key features include AI-driven virtual try-on that maps garments onto diverse model images with realistic fabric draping, fit, and texture, multi-category support covering tops, dresses, pants, outerwear, and accessories, customizable model selection allowing shoppers to view items on models matching their body type and skin tone, and seamless e-commerce integration through plugins and APIs for major platforms including Shopify and custom storefronts. The platform also provides analytics on try-on engagement and conversion impact.

Veesual offers a one-month free trial, with the Standard plan starting at $50 per month and custom enterprise pricing for larger implementations. It is best suited for fashion e-commerce brands looking to reduce return rates, online retailers seeking to increase conversion and average order value, apparel marketplaces wanting to improve the shopping experience, and direct-to-consumer fashion startups competing with established brands on customer experience.
