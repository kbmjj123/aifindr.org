Zapier AI is the AI-powered evolution of the popular automation platform, connecting 6000+ apps to create powerful workflows. Its AI features enable more intelligent automations that can make decisions, generate content, and extract insights.

Key AI capabilities include AI-powered content generation within Zaps, intelligent data extraction from unstructured content, AI decision steps that route data based on content analysis, and natural language creation of Zaps. These features allow users to build automations that previously required custom programming.

Zapier AI makes sophisticated automation accessible to non-technical users, enabling them to build workflows that handle complex, unstructured data and make intelligent decisions.
