Tableau has integrated AI capabilities throughout its business intelligence platform. Tableau AI makes data analysis more accessible by allowing users to ask questions in natural language and receive automated insights.

Key AI features include Ask Data for natural language querying of datasets, Explain Data for automatic insight generation about specific data points, and Einstein Discovery for predictive analytics and automated forecasting. These features make complex data analysis accessible to non-technical users.

Tableau remains the industry leader in data visualization, and its AI features are designed to help all users, regardless of technical skill, gain deeper insights from their data faster.
