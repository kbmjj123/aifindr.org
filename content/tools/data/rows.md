Rows is an AI-powered spreadsheet platform that transforms traditional spreadsheets into intelligent data analysis and business automation tools. Unlike conventional spreadsheet applications, Rows integrates AI directly into the grid, allowing users to analyze data, generate content, classify information, and extract insights using natural language prompts within their spreadsheet cells. The platform combines the familiarity of spreadsheet interfaces with the power of AI, built-in data integrations, and automated workflows.

Key features include AI functions that can summarize text, classify data, generate content, and perform analysis directly within spreadsheet formulas, one-click integrations with over 50 business platforms including Google Analytics, Stripe, Salesforce, and social media APIs, automated data refresh that keeps connected data current without manual imports, and shareable dashboards and reports with interactive charts. The platform also includes a no-code automation builder that triggers actions based on spreadsheet changes and supports team collaboration with granular permission controls.

Rows offers a free tier with basic features and limited AI tasks, with Plus and Pro subscriptions providing expanded access and unlimited AI usage. It is best suited for marketers analyzing campaign data across platforms, sales teams tracking pipeline metrics, startup operators building lightweight internal tools, and business professionals who need spreadsheet-based analysis with AI augmentation and live data connections.
