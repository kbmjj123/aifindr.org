Relativity is an AI-powered eDiscovery and data investigation platform that revolutionizes how legal professionals manage complex data discovery and build case strategies. The platform provides a secure, end-to-end solution for collecting, processing, reviewing, and producing electronically stored information during litigation, investigations, and regulatory compliance matters. Relativity's AI capabilities accelerate document review, surface key evidence, and help legal teams develop data-driven case strategies.

Key features include AI-assisted document review with technology-assisted review (TAR) and active learning that prioritizes the most relevant documents for human review, advanced analytics for communication pattern analysis and concept clustering, automated redaction and privilege identification to protect sensitive information, and customizable workflows that adapt to different case types and review methodologies. The platform also provides robust security controls, detailed audit trails, and support for multi-party and multi-jurisdictional matters.

Relativity offers custom pricing based on deployment scope and organizational needs, with a free trial available for evaluation. It is best suited for law firms managing large-scale litigation and investigations, corporate legal departments handling internal investigations and regulatory responses, government agencies conducting complex data-intensive inquiries, and eDiscovery service providers delivering managed review services to their clients.
