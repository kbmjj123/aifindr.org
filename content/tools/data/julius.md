Julius AI is a conversational data analysis platform that makes working with data as simple as having a conversation. Users upload their data files in formats like CSV, Excel, or Google Sheets, and can start asking questions, requesting visualizations, or performing analysis in natural language.

The AI understands the data structure and can perform complex analyses including statistical tests, regressions, time series forecasting, and machine learning. It automatically generates visualizations and explanations of the results in plain language.

Julius AI is designed for analysts, researchers, and business professionals who want to analyze data quickly without writing code or using complex statistical software.
