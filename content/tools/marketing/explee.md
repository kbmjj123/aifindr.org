Explee is an AI-powered B2B lead generation platform that transforms simple customer descriptions into verified, actionable lead lists. Instead of spending hours manually researching potential clients, sales teams and marketers can describe their ideal customer in plain language, and Explee's AI engine automatically identifies matching companies and contacts from its extensive database. The platform cross-references multiple data sources to verify contact information and enrich lead profiles with relevant business intelligence.

Key features include natural language lead discovery where users describe target customers conversationally, automated lead verification to ensure data accuracy, enrichment of lead profiles with company size, industry, and technology stack details, and seamless export to popular CRM and sales engagement platforms. The AI continuously learns from user feedback and conversion data to refine its matching algorithms over time, delivering increasingly precise results with continued use.

Explee operates on a paid subscription model, with pricing tailored to organization size and lead volume requirements. It is best suited for B2B sales teams, account-based marketing professionals, recruitment agencies, and business development representatives who need to efficiently build targeted prospect lists without manual data gathering.
