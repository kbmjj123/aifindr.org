Trade Ideas is an AI-powered trading platform that revolutionizes how traders analyze markets and make decisions. The tool uses advanced machine learning algorithms to scan thousands of stocks in real time, identifying high-probability trading opportunities based on technical patterns, momentum indicators, and market sentiment. Its flagship feature, Holly AI, is an autonomous trading assistant that continuously evaluates market conditions and suggests actionable trade setups.

Key features include real-time market scanning across multiple exchanges, AI-generated trade signals with entry and exit points, backtesting capabilities to validate strategies against historical data, and customizable alert systems that notify traders of emerging opportunities. The platform also offers simulated trading environments where users can practice without risking capital, as well as detailed performance analytics to track and improve trading results.

Trade Ideas offers tiered pricing starting at $118 per month for the Standard plan, with the Premium plan at $228 per month including priority support and exclusive educational content. A free trial is available for exploration. The platform is best suited for active day traders, swing traders, and quantitative investors who want to leverage AI to enhance their market analysis and decision-making process.
