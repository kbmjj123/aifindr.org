Insta Headshots is an AI-powered photography tool that transforms ordinary selfies into polished, professional-grade headshots in minutes. Using advanced generative AI and facial enhancement algorithms, the platform takes user-uploaded photos and produces studio-quality portraits suitable for LinkedIn profiles, company websites, resumes, and professional social media presence. The AI intelligently adjusts lighting, removes background distractions, refines skin tones, and offers multiple outfit and background style options.

Key features include rapid processing that delivers finished headshots in approximately 15 minutes, multiple look variations from a single upload, customizable backgrounds ranging from corporate office to outdoor settings, and AI-driven facial retouching that enhances without appearing artificial. Users can choose from various professional styles and receive up to 50 headshot variations with 5 different looks. The pay-only-if-satisfied model ensures users only pay for results they genuinely like.

Insta Headshots starts at $59 per session, which includes 50 headshots across 5 distinct looks. The platform is best suited for job seekers updating their professional profiles, remote workers needing consistent team photos, entrepreneurs building their personal brand, and anyone who needs a quality headshot without the time and expense of a professional photography session.
