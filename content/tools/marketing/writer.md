Writer is an enterprise-focused AI writing and content platform that prioritizes brand consistency and governance. Unlike general-purpose AI tools, Writer is designed to produce content that aligns with specific brand guidelines, voice, and style requirements.

The platform includes a powerful AI writing assistant, a customizable LLM that can be fine-tuned on company data, and guardrails to ensure generated content meets compliance requirements. Writer's Knowledge Graph understands relationships between products, features, and terminology unique to each organization.

Writer is used by large enterprises for marketing content, product documentation, customer communications, and internal knowledge management where brand consistency and accuracy are critical.
