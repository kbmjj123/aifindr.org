VidAU is an AI-powered video advertising platform that transforms product ideas and descriptions into high-converting video ads. The tool uses generative AI to automate the entire video ad creation process, from script writing and scene composition to voiceover and animation. Users simply input their product details, target audience, and preferred style, and VidAU generates multiple video ad variations optimized for different platforms and campaign objectives.

Key features include AI script generation that creates compelling ad copy tailored to product features and audience preferences, automated scene creation with product visualization and animated elements, multi-language voiceover synthesis with natural-sounding AI voices, and platform-specific formatting that optimizes video dimensions and pacing for TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, and Facebook ads. The platform also supports A/B testing by generating multiple creative variations from a single product brief, helping marketers identify top-performing ad concepts faster.

VidAU operates on a paid subscription model with pricing based on usage volume and feature access. It is best suited for e-commerce brands and DTC companies running paid social campaigns, dropshippers needing rapid ad creative production, performance marketing agencies managing multiple client accounts, and product marketers who want to test video ad concepts without the cost and turnaround time of traditional video production.
