Topaz Labs is a professional-grade AI photo and video enhancement software suite that processes all media locally on the user's machine, ensuring data security and privacy. The platform offers three flagship products: Photo AI for image enhancement and restoration, Video AI for video upscaling and frame interpolation, and Gigapixel AI for specialized image upscaling. Unlike cloud-based alternatives, Topaz Labs performs all AI processing on local hardware, making it suitable for sensitive content and offline workflows.

Key features include AI-powered noise reduction that cleans up low-light and high-ISO images while preserving detail, intelligent upscaling that increases resolution up to 6x without introducing artifacts, face recovery technology that reconstructs facial features in low-quality source material, frame interpolation for smooth slow-motion creation from standard footage, and stabilization for handheld and shaky video. The software also provides batch processing capabilities, allowing users to enhance multiple files simultaneously with consistent settings.

Topaz Labs offers one-time purchase pricing: Photo AI at $199, Video AI at $299, and Gigapixel AI at $99, with free trials available for each product. The software runs on both Windows and macOS. It is best suited for professional photographers restoring archival images, videographers upscaling footage for higher-resolution deliverables, digital artists preparing assets for print, and anyone working with sensitive visual content who requires local, secure AI processing.
