Audiosocket is a music licensing platform that streamlines the process of finding, licensing, and using high-quality music tracks for creative projects. The platform offers a curated library of pre-cleared music spanning multiple genres, moods, and instrumentation styles, making it easy for content creators to discover the perfect soundtrack without navigating complex licensing negotiations. Audiosocket's AI-powered search and recommendation engine helps users find tracks that match the emotional tone and pacing of their content.

Key features include a vast catalog of licensable music from independent artists and composers, AI-driven music discovery that suggests tracks based on mood, genre, tempo, and project context, simplified licensing options covering commercial use, broadcast, digital media, and film, and direct integration with popular video editing and content creation workflows. The platform handles all rights management and artist compensation, ensuring legal compliance while supporting the creative community. Tracks are available in high-quality formats suitable for professional productions.

Audiosocket operates on a paid licensing model with pricing varying by usage type and scope, and offers a trial option for exploration. It is best suited for video producers, filmmakers, podcasters, advertising agencies, game developers, and social media content creators who need legally cleared, high-quality music without the complexity and cost of traditional music licensing.
