Kits is an AI-powered music creation platform that revolutionizes audio production with advanced voice generation and vocal processing capabilities. The tool enables musicians, producers, and content creators to generate realistic AI singing voices, create vocal harmonies, and isolate or remove vocals from existing tracks. Kits' voice library includes a diverse range of vocal styles and characteristics, allowing users to experiment with different voices for their productions without hiring session vocalists.

Key features include AI voice generation that creates realistic singing performances from melody input, vocal isolation and removal for creating instrumental versions or acapellas, voice conversion that transforms one vocal style into another while preserving the original performance's emotion and timing, and a royalty-free voice library that can be used in commercial productions. The platform also offers stem separation for isolating individual instruments, pitch correction and tuning, and integration with popular digital audio workstations through file export.

Kits offers a free tier for getting started, with multiple subscription plans available for different usage levels and feature access. It is best suited for independent musicians and producers creating demo tracks, content creators needing custom vocal elements, beatmakers looking to add vocal hooks, and audio engineers who need efficient vocal processing tools without the overhead of traditional studio recording sessions.
