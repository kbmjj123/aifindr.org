Magnific (formerly Freepik) AI Voice Generator is the voiceover component of Magnific’s creative AI suite, focused on turning text into natural speech for production-ready content. It uses ElevenLabs’ advanced models via the Magnific API to create realistic voiceovers for videos, podcasts, presentations, and other media. Aimed primarily at developers, creative teams, and studios already building with Magnific, it provides granular control over how a voice sounds while fitting into broader image and video workflows on the same platform.
