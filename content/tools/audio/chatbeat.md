Chatbeat is an AI-powered brand visibility platform that helps businesses understand, monitor, and improve how their brand appears within AI-powered search engines and chatbot responses. As more consumers turn to AI assistants like ChatGPT, Claude, and Google's AI overviews for information, Chatbeat provides the tools to track brand mentions, analyze sentiment, and optimize digital presence for AI-driven discovery. The platform bridges the gap between traditional SEO and the emerging field of AI answer engine optimization.

Key features include real-time monitoring of how AI models reference a brand across different platforms and queries, sentiment analysis that tracks the tone and accuracy of AI-generated brand mentions, competitive benchmarking to compare AI visibility against industry peers, and actionable optimization recommendations that help improve brand representation in AI training data and responses. The platform also provides trend tracking over time, alerting users to significant changes in AI perception of their brand.

Chatbeat operates on a paid subscription model with a free trial available for evaluation. It is best suited for brand marketers, PR professionals, SEO specialists, and digital strategists who recognize that consumer discovery is increasingly mediated by AI and want to ensure their brand narrative remains accurate and favorable across all AI platforms.
